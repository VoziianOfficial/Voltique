Clear platform role

Not a Direct Contractor

Voltique does not perform electrical work directly and does not represent itself as an electrician company, installer, repair company, inspector, manufacturer, or retailer. The platform helps users submit electrical request details and compare available local provider options. Final pricing, scheduling, warranties, licenses, insurance, service terms, and work quality are handled by participating independent providers.
