Overview

Voltique may use cookies and similar technologies to operate this website, remember cookie banner preferences, support contact-form functionality, improve user experience, understand general website usage, and help protect the site from spam or misuse.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files that may be stored on a user's browser or device when a website is visited. Cookies can help a website remember preferences, maintain basic functionality, measure website usage, and support security or anti-spam features.

Similar technologies may include local storage, session storage, pixels, tags, scripts, device identifiers, and browser-based preference storage.

Essential cookies and storage may be used to make the website work properly, remember cookie banner choices, support navigation, protect forms, and maintain basic website functionality.

Performance and Analytics Cookies

Security and Anti-Spam Cookies

Security tools may use cookies or similar technologies to help detect spam, suspicious submissions, automated behavior, or misuse of the request form.

Advertising or Partner Cookies

Voltique may use advertising, call-routing, lead-processing, or partner tools that use cookies or similar technologies to understand request activity, measure campaign performance, or support provider-matching operations.

Local Storage and Browser Preferences

This website may use browser local storage to remember non-sensitive preferences, such as whether a user has accepted the cookie banner. Local storage stays on the browser until it is cleared by the user, browser settings, or website logic.

Clearing browser data may reset cookie preferences and cause the cookie banner to appear again.

Analytics and Site Improvement

Your Cookie Choices

Users can control cookies through browser settings. Most browsers allow users to block, delete, or manage cookies and site data. Some browsers also provide privacy controls for tracking prevention or third-party cookies.

  • Blocking cookies may affect website functionality.
  • Deleting cookies may reset saved preferences.
  • Browser settings may not block every type of local storage or similar technology.
  • Some essential cookies or storage may be necessary for form security and site operation.

Users who do not want non-essential cookies should adjust browser settings or avoid using non-essential site features.
