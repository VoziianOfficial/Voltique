Overview

Voltique is an independent provider-matching platform. Voltique does not perform electrical work directly and does not act as a licensed electrical contractor, installer, inspector, or repair provider. Information submitted through the site may be used to help process request details and connect users with participating independent providers.

Information We May Collect

When a user submits a request, contacts the request desk, uses a form, or interacts with this website, Voltique may collect information such as:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and contact preferences.
  • Electrical request category, project details, property access notes, and timing preferences.
  • Information voluntarily included in message fields or request descriptions.
  • Technical information such as browser type, device type, IP address, general usage data, and page interactions.
  • Consent records, form source page, submission timestamps, and anti-spam validation data.

Users should not submit sensitive personal information unless it is necessary for the request. Voltique does not request payment card numbers through the standard contact form.

How Information May Be Used

Information submitted through Voltique may be used for the following purposes:

  • To receive, review, and organize request details.
  • To respond to user inquiries and provide request-related communication.
  • To help match users with participating independent providers.
  • To share request details with providers who may be able to discuss availability, pricing, scheduling, and service terms.
  • To improve website performance, usability, security, fraud prevention, and form reliability.
  • To comply with applicable legal, security, and operational requirements.

Voltique does not guarantee that a provider will be available, qualified for every project type, or able to offer specific pricing or scheduling.

Information Sharing

Request information may be shared with participating independent providers, service partners, website support vendors, communication tools, security tools, hosting providers, and other operational vendors when reasonably necessary to process a request or operate the website.

Participating Providers

By submitting a request, users understand that their request details may be shared with participating providers so those providers may contact the user, review the request, and provide their own pricing, availability, service scope, and terms.

Legal or Safety Needs

Voltique may disclose information when required by law, to protect rights and safety, to investigate misuse, to prevent fraud, or to respond to lawful requests.

Cookies and Website Data

This website may use cookies, local storage, analytics tools, security tools, and similar technologies to support website functionality, remember cookie preferences, understand page usage, and improve the user experience.

Users can control cookies through browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality, such as cookie banner preferences or form-related behavior.

Your Choices

Depending on applicable law and the information involved, users may request access, correction, deletion, or limitation of certain personal information. Users may also request that Voltique stop using submitted contact information for request follow-up, subject to legal and operational requirements.

To make a privacy-related request, contact Voltique using the information listed in the contact section below. Voltique may need to verify the request before taking action.

Security

Voltique uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect submitted information. However, no website, email transmission, hosting system, or internet-based service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Users should avoid submitting unnecessary sensitive information through forms or email.

Children's Privacy

Voltique is intended for adults seeking electrical provider-matching information. The website is not directed to children, and Voltique does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

Voltique may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the “Last updated” date on this page. Continued use of the website after an update means the user accepts the revised policy.

Important Platform Disclaimer

Voltique is an independent provider-matching platform and does not perform electrical services directly. Any final pricing, scheduling, inspection, diagnosis, installation, repair, permit handling, warranties, licenses, insurance, and service terms are handled by the independent provider selected by the user.
